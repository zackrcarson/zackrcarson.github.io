Personal Game Development Demo: Laser Defender

Defend your planet! This top-down stile space shooter game was a blast to make, and even more fun to play! Move with "wasd" and shoot with your left mouse button. Hit ESC to pause the game, and make sure to pick up the pills to heal yourself (especially the red ones)! See if you can make it to the first boss before you die.. Good luck!
